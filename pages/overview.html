Executive Summary

As of now, data coordinators—the administrators most responsible for data processing to aid improvement—who use Renaissance Learning, Inc.’s Star 360 assessment package have access to a wealth of student growth data. They use data from Star to inform decision making and improve educational quality within a school or district. However, Renaissance has identified that data coordinators need to compare students’ achievement while taking their demographic contexts into account, an ability currently not available in Renaissance’s platform.

To validate and address these needs, Renaissance Learning has partnered with Enlighten, a student consulting team from Carnegie Mellon University’s Masters in Education Technology and Applied Learning Science (METALS).
